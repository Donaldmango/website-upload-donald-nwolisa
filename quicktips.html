Everyday Eco-Friendly Tips

  • Switch to LED Bulbs: They use up to 80% less energy than incandescent bulbs.
  • Bring Your Own Bags: Say no to plastic and use reusable shopping bags.
  • Unplug Devices: Even when off, plugged-in electronics can still draw power.
  • Collect Rainwater: Use it to water plants and save on your water bill.
  • Eat Local: Buying local reduces transportation emissions and supports your community.
  • Compost Organic Waste: Reduce landfill load and create nutrient-rich soil.
  • Use Refillable Water Bottles: Cut down on single-use plastics and stay hydrated sustainably.
  • Go Paperless: Opt for digital receipts and e-bills whenever possible.
